# Mushroom-TRACKR-AI
An ML model that predicts where mushrooms will pop up

How to use:
Download the contents in the "dist" folder. It should be at the top of the list.
Place all files in a folder
Unzip the datasets and executable by clicking. WARNING! The datasets total at 2 gigabytes. Make sure your device can handle it. It may be slow to unzip.
Once finished, go into dist and double-click the executable.
Your terminal should pop up with a message. Wait 30 seconds afterwards for another window to pop up.
Please be patient as the second window loads.
